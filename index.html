Justice Amy Coney Barrett

Personal Life


Amy Coney Barret was born in New Orleans, Louisiana with 5 sisters and a brother. She went to an all girls high school and then went to Rhodes College majoring in English Literature. She graduated named most outstanding English student. Then she went to Notre Dame Law School on a full-ride scholarship. She graduated Law school with a summa cum laude. In 1999 she married Jesse M. Barret and they had 5 children and adopted 2. She is also a Catholic.
